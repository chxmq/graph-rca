# Reddit Incident

**Category:** Infrastructure
**Severity:** Critical
**Source:** https://web.archive.org/web/20221029203405/https://www.reddit.com/r/announcements/comments/4y0m56/why_reddit_was_down_on_aug_11/

## Description

Experienced an outage for 1.5 hours, followed by another 1.5 hours of degraded performance on Thursday August 11 2016. This was due to an error during a migration of a critical backend system.

## Root Cause

An error during a migration of a critical backend system
