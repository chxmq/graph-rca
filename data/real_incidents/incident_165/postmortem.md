# Shapeshift Incident

**Category:** Security
**Severity:** Medium
**Source:** http://web.archive.org/web/20160610080136/https://www.scribd.com/doc/309574927/ShapeShift-Post-Mortem-Public

## Description

Poor security practices enabled an employee to steal $200,000 in cryptocurrency in 3 separate hacks over a 1 month period. The company's CEO expanded upon the story in a [blog post](https://web.archive.org/web/20190811214903/http://moneyandstate.com:80/looting-of-the-fox/).

## Root Cause

Poor security practices enabled an employee to steal $200,000 in cryptocurrency in 3 separate hacks over a 1 month period
